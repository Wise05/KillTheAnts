Overview: 
Puzzle platformer that involves killing all ants before buring down the most of the house. 

Objects:
Burning tools: 
matches, lasers, and bombs.
Ways to put out fires:
walking on fire and water bucket (worried about this one's mechanics).
Other objects: 
wooden blocks, stone blocks, ladders, chests, mirrors (lasers), stairs. 
Types of ants: 
stationary, ranged, aggressive, and free spirits
Electrical stuff:
levelers, lights?, 

Mechanics:
Fire spread: fire burns flamable objects at a certain rate only if adjacent
Carbon monoxide levels rise unless there is a large enough hole in the roof
